ABOUT SRI PRAKASH EDU

SRI PRAKASH EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTES , where the dreams of students and parents come true.

SRI PRAKASH EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTES cordially invite you to explore the academic world of SRI PRAKASH, being led by Mr. Ch.V.K.Narasimha rao, a self-made man, the best counselor, an exemplary leader, a commendable administrator with uncompromising commitment to excellence. He is an epitome of trust and inspiration to everyone who wishes to achieve success in personal and professional life as well. We believe that the fast changing world requires knowledgeable professionals with moral values who can withstand the dynamic future world.

We provide IIT foundation for all our students from schooling. We are proud to share that we are able to send one among every three of our students from each batch to different well-known medical colleges. We have shown remarkable progress in the academic arena of all our students by sending most of them to the most prestigious IITs, NITs, IIITs and BITs as it is a far-fetched dream of many students from rural areas also with the indelible support given by parents, well-wishers, adequate experienced teaching and non-teaching staff.

We believe that IIT & MEDICAL ACADEMY should nurture values with wisdom among students. The reason behind our successful journey over a decade is taking utmost care about every individual student by showering unconditional and parental love and producing the desired results every year and it continues.

OUR VISION & MISSION

SRI PRAKASH EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTES founded for rural community education, our mission is to discover, examine, transmit and apply knowledge that contributes to the positive development and well-being of individuals, organizations and society.

We provide an outstanding education at school, undergraduate, graduate, and professional education level to promising, qualified students in order to prepare them to contribute fully to society as globally engaged citizen leaders. Sasi Educational Institutes is dedicated to preparing students to excel in an increasingly advanced and interconnected global society.
